Our Purpose & Goal

The purpose of the College People Connect is to provide college students with meaningful and impactful social and professional connections The website itself will provide students with last-minute activities in the area that other college students will be involved with, networking opportunities, and a blog-style space where students can share opportunities and what they are currently working on as well as the latest news in the area . The overall goal of this website is to connect students with more social experiences, encourage engagement, and get students connected with people in their major, or across different majors within different universities.

Who We are & Why

We are a platform that is community-based to reduce isolation and to help college students build connections throughout campuses. We believe that firm social connections can lead to more student siccess, greater graduation rates, and a overall great college experiance.
